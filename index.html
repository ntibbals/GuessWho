My Journey

Born in Spokane Valley Washington, I made the decision to relocate to the Greater Seattle area in 2012. Since then, I have fallen in love with the Puget Sound. From the water and mountain views down to city skyline, the dynamic culture of the Puget Sound area has everything you could ask for. Since moving to the area, I have the wonderful opportunity to meet some fantastic people and be part of some incredible organizations. I hope to continue to build upon that as I continue to grow and develop both personally and professionally.
